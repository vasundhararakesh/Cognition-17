Random Dot Motion Task

In this experiment, you will see several dots moving about on the screen. Your goal is to identify if the overall pattern of dots is moving to the left or the right. You will do this several times, for dot displays with different movement patterns. You can take as long as you like to decide the direction of motion within each trial, and as long as you like to rest up between two trials. We don't expect the experiment to take longer than 25 minutes to complete.
